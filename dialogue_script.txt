3D EYE EXAM SIMULATOR - DIALOGUE SCRIPT
======================================
This file lists all the lines of spoken dialogue for the Doctor (Clinician) and the Patient.
Use these lines to record voice assets (e.g. via ElevenLabs) for the simulator.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. DOCTOR SELECT SCREEN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

MALE DOCTOR:
"Hello. I am Dr. Allen Watts. Let's get started."

FEMALE DOCTOR:
"Hello. I am Dr. Lauren Mays. Let's get started."


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Hello doctor, the patient's ready for you."

"Hello! I am Dr. Allen Watts. Let's head back to the pre-testing room for some initial measurements."

DOCTOR (Dr. Lauren Mays):
"Hello! I am Dr. Lauren Mays. Let's head back to the pre-testing room for some initial measurements."

PATIENT:
"Sounds good, doctor. Lead the way."


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. AUTOREFRACTOR VIEWPORT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DOCTOR:
"Please place your chin on the rest and look into the screen. Keep looking at the hot air balloon."

DOCTOR (Eye Switch):
"Perfect. Now looking at your left eye, keep looking at the balloon."

DOCTOR (Finished):
"Objective measurements complete. You can step back."


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. NCT TONOMETER VIEWPORT (AIR PUFF)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DOCTOR:
"Now look at the small red dot in the center of the ring. You'll feel a tiny puff of air."

DOCTOR (Eye Switch):
"Don't blink, now checking the left eye."

DOCTOR (Finished):
"All done with eye pressure, thank you."


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
